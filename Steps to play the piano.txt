Steps to play the piano

1. connect the nicla vison to laptop using USB.
2. upload the model(main.py + trained.tflite + labels) to nicla vision drive.
3. reconnect the nicla vision to laptop using USB.
4. Run the play_piano.py file in terminal in the same folder where all the piano audio keys are.
5. Show your fist in front of the nicla vision camera to activate the piano
6. move your fingers and play the piano
